We are an uptempo riposte to the absurdity of life and the certainty of death, drawing inspiration from the blood-soaked Northern European and North American ballad traditions and the chilling ‘Danse Macabre’ imagery of medieval Europe. We set traditional British, Irish and American songs and our own compositions to lively old-time instrumentation.
